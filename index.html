About Us

At KenThreads, we specialize in precision automotive bolt threading and rethreading. From creating new threads for bolts to expertly drilling out and rethreading broken or damaged bolts, we ensure your car parts are securely fastened for peak performance. Trust KenThreads to keep your vehicle running smoothly with reliable and professional threading solutions.

Our Services

Bonnet

Under the bonnet, KenThreads delivers top-tier threading services for critical components. From rethreading damaged bolts to threading new ones, we ensure that every connection is as strong and reliable as the vehicle itself, providing peace of mind and lasting durability.

Engine

Our expertise extends to the heart of your vehicle—its engine. KenThreads offers specialized threading solutions for engine components, ensuring that every bolt is perfectly fitted and secure, contributing to the smooth and powerful performance of your engine.

KenThreads ensures your wheels are securely fastened with precision bolt threading and rethreading services. Whether it's installing new threads or repairing damaged ones, we guarantee your wheels are road-ready, providing safety and stability on every journey.
